Proflight simulator is excellent software you can use to simulate flying all over the world from the comfort of your home. Simulator has more than 20 000 world airports built in, has real terrain and more than 120 airplanes to fly with. You can experience flight on all kind of planes from 1903 Wright flyer to modern combat fighter jets. You can land at 20 000 world airports receiving latest weather forecast and NASA flight models. You will see the every time updated terrain map based on the information provided by Defense Mapping Agency. This flight simulator is used not only in games but also in various flight schools and TV episodes. Everything (world terrain, jet reactions, planetary alignments and movements) in this Proflight simulator game resembles real flight scenario and is based on real data. You will see the real world (cars, lights and etc) from the cockpit of your jet. You will have full control over your flight. The flight simulator is a professional guide to learn how to fly with real jetliner. The scenery and airports can be added on demand. With this simulator you can fly over all 6 continents, even over your home. You can change aircrafts to fly with. The set of equipment allows you to track location of heaven object in real time even coinciding with your pc clock. If it is daytime in New York during the flight the simulator shows daytime too. Every detail is taken in consideration even 24 hour daytime or nighttime in the poles during the certain season period. Military world data gives you the precision to details in terms of terrain map. In terms of weather you can control it yourself experiencing flight in stormy or still weather environment or you can use real live weather scenario too. All data is available in Proflight simulator: distance to airport, dew point, temperature, the strength of the wind and also the forecast of the aforementioned ones. You can experience take-offs and touch-downs of various jets from old to new and from military fighter jets to medical helicopter. You can also fly with other Proflight simulator users can locate them using Google maps and participate in air shows and have fun this way.
